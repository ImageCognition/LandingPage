The Internet Is Broken

There are 32 million people with visual impairments. Many rely on a Screen Reader to browse the internet.

Screen Readers need alt-tags to provide a caption for images. However, for the majority of photos on the internet they are missing.

We increasingly depend on images.

Lets stop leaving 32 million people behind.

Who's in photos or what the text says.

All user uploaded content is inaccessible.

The Solution

The latest advancements in machine learning enable us to generate captions for every image on the internet. These captions tell you what's in the photo, who they are and what their expression is.
